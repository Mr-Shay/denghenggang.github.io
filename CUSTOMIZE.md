# Customize

This file allows you to customize the settings and configurations for your project.

## Available Options

1. **Theme Selection:** You can choose your preferred theme.
2. **Layout Customization:** Modify the layout as per your requirements.
3. **Feature Enabling/Disabling:** Enable or disable features based on your needs.

## Instructions

Follow the guidelines below to make the necessary changes:
- Ensure that you understand the options before making changes.
- Keep backups of previous configurations.
- Test your customization before going live.
